Points that apply to all exercises

All exercises should be performed in a quiet & dark room.

Before doing any of the exercises you should take the following steps

  1. find a comfortable position.
  2. take a few deep breaths
  3. let the mind calm down by staying still for 2 minutes

Preliminary Exercise 2

Overview

  • The goal of this exercise is to build body awareness by making mental contact with different parts of the body.
  • When making mental contact with a part of the body it may help to imagine looking at that part of the body (while keeping your eyes closed).
  • If it is difficult to make mental contact with that part of the body you can also try noticing the sensation of the breath in that part of the body. I have found thin to be particularly useful for the shoulders, back of the neck, chest and and all other points along the center line of the body.
  • For each step repeat the phrase “Calm & Relaxed” for 5 breaths.
  • Breathe normally.

Steps

  1. Right hand to right shoulder
  2. Left hand to left shoulder
  3. Shoulders
  4. Jaw
    1. Notice if upper and lower teeth are touching.
    2. Let your jaw sag.
    3. Exhale through slightly parted lips.
  5. Chest.
  6. Abdominal region.
  7. Lower back. (5 to 8 breaths if you have back pain)
  8. Right thigh to knee.
  9. Right knee to foot.
  10. Right foot.
  11. Entire right leg.
  12. Left thigh to knee.
  13. Left knee to foot.
  14. Entire left leg.
