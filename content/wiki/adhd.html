Delay Research

While learning something in a book I want to look up more information or have questions I want answered. This can be distracting from the reading, stopping looking thing up, no guarantee on when I a coming back.

And it might turn out I'm not really interested in this. Just in that moment I was. So I will try to delay research. Write down the question if you must and see if its interesting a month from now. If so then research it.
