## Services

I offer tutoring to students grades 5th and up. All lessons are conducted via zoom. My cost is $50 per hour per student. 

In terms of what material I teach, I prefer to teach those with less than a year of programming experience either [Pyret](https://www.pyret.org/) or A variant of Racket called  [beginner student language (BSL)](https://docs.racket-lang.org/htdp-langs/beginner.html). For more on why I prefer these types of languages see [Pedagogical Languages]({{< relref "/posts/pl.md" >}}).

My intermediate students usually learn Java since that is what is used for the AP computer science exam, however I can also teach Swift, Javascript, and Python.
